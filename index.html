How soon can I expect recurring income?
Most creators see their first pledges within days when they promote their launch to engaged followers. Use the income planner above to stay realistic about supporter conversion.
What should I offer in my first tier?
Start with lightweight community perks: exclusive updates, polls, or access to a Discord channel. As you understand your audience, layer in richer content or live sessions.
How many tiers are ideal?
Most successful creators experiment with 2–3 tiers. Keep pricing steps meaningful and clearly communicate the jump in value between each level.

Yes! Introduce loyalty bonuses like annual meetups, exclusive merch, or mentorship calls unlocked after consecutive months. The tier presets above include a few ready-to-use ideas.
